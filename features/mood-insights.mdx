The Insights page provides powerful visualization tools to help you understand your emotional patterns and track your mental wellbeing over time.

## Accessing Insights

Click the "Insights" option in the sidebar navigation to open the Insights page. You'll see a two-panel layout:

- **Left panel** - Entry list or mood calendar
- **Right panel** - Emotion summary statistics and selected entry preview

## View modes

Insights offers two complementary ways to explore your journal data:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Informative view">
    Displays your entries in a list format with emotion badges, perfect for browsing content chronologically and reading entry previews.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Visual view">
    Shows a color-coded calendar where each day is represented by a colored square indicating that day's detected emotion.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

Switch between views using the "Informative" and "Visual" buttons at the top of the page.

## Informative view

### Entry cards

Each entry is displayed as a card showing:

- **Date** - When the entry was written
- **Emotion badge** - Color-coded emotion label
- **Content preview** - First few lines of your entry

```javascript
// From InsightsPage.jsx:74-78
```

### Emotion-coded borders

Each card features a colored left border corresponding to the detected emotion, making it easy to scan your emotional journey at a glance.

<Info>
Click any entry card to view its full content in the preview panel on the right.
</Info>

## Visual view (Mood calendar)

### Calendar granularity

The calendar can display your data in two ways:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Month view">
    Shows a traditional monthly calendar for the selected month and year. Each date is a colored square representing that day's emotion.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Year view">
    Displays all 12 months of the selected year in a scrollable layout, perfect for seeing long-term patterns.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

Toggle between month and year view using the switch at the top of the calendar.

### Calendar navigation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select month (month view only)">
    Use the month dropdown to jump to any month in the selected year.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select year">
    Use the year dropdown to view any year that contains journal entries (plus 3 years before and after).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Explore dates">
    Hover over any colored square to see the date and emotion in a tooltip.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View entry">
    Click any square with a journal entry to load it in the preview panel.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Color coding

Each emotion is represented by a distinct color:

- **Joy** - Bright yellow/gold
- **Sadness** - Blue
- **Anger** - Red
- **Fear** - Purple
- **Disgust** - Green
- **Surprise** - Orange
- **Neutral** - Gray
- **No entry** - Light background with dotted border

<Note>
Days without journal entries appear with a semi-transparent background and cannot be clicked.
</Note>

## Entry preview panel

When you click an entry (from either view mode), the preview panel shows:

- **Entry date** - Formatted date header
- **Emotion badge** - Color-coded emotion label
- **Content preview** - First five lines of the entry
- **View Entry button** - Opens the full entry in the main journal view

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click to preview">
    Click any entry card or calendar square with a journal entry.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review content">
    Read the date, emotion, and content preview in the right panel.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View full entry (optional)">
    Click "View Entry" to open the complete entry with all suggestions and the ability to edit.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Close preview">
    Click the X icon to clear the preview and select another entry.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Insights for mental health tracking

### Pattern recognition

Use the calendar view to identify:

- **Recurring patterns** - Do you experience certain emotions on specific days of the week?
- **Seasonal trends** - Are there times of year when you feel consistently better or worse?
- **Clusters** - Are difficult periods isolated or do they come in waves?
- **Progress** - Can you see improvement over weeks or months?

<Tip>
Switch to year view to spot long-term trends that might not be visible in month view.
</Tip>

### Using the data

The emotion statistics help you:

1. **Understand your baseline** - What emotions do you experience most frequently?
2. **Track changes** - Are you becoming more positive over time?
3. **Identify triggers** - What external factors correlate with emotional shifts?
4. **Celebrate progress** - Recognize periods of consistent positive emotions

<Warning>
While Insights provides valuable data for self-reflection, it should not replace professional mental health support. If you're struggling, please consult with a qualified healthcare provider.
</Warning>

## Interactive features

### Hover tooltips

Hover over any calendar date to see:
- The formatted date (e.g., "January 15, 2024")
- The detected emotion for that day

### Click interactions

- **Entry cards** - Opens preview in right panel
- **Calendar squares** (with entries) - Opens preview in right panel
- **View Entry button** - Navigates to full entry view
- **Close button** - Clears the current preview

### Responsive design

The Insights page adapts to different screen sizes:

- **Desktop** - Side-by-side panels for easy comparison
- **Mobile/tablet** - Stacked panels for optimal viewing
