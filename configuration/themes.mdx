MoodJourney offers multiple theme options to match your aesthetic preferences and environment. Choose from light, dark, system, or the unique girlboss theme.

## Available themes

### Light theme

A clean, minimalist light theme with a soft color palette:

- **Primary color**: Navy blue (`#23325A`)
- **Background**: Warm off-white (`#F3EEEB`)
- **Paper/cards**: Creamy white (`#FFFCF9`)
- **Text**: Dark navy for high contrast

Ideal for daytime use and well-lit environments.

### Dark theme

A sophisticated dark theme for reduced eye strain:

- **Primary color**: Warm beige (`#F3EEEB`)
- **Background**: Deep navy (`#1A2238`)
- **Paper/cards**: Rich blue (`#23325A`)
- **Text**: Light beige for readability

Perfect for nighttime journaling or low-light conditions.

### System theme

Automatically switches between light and dark themes based on your operating system's color scheme preference.

<Note>
  System theme uses the `prefers-color-scheme` media query to detect your OS settings. Changes are applied immediately when your system theme changes.
</Note>

### Girlboss theme

A vibrant, empowering theme with playful aesthetics:

- **Primary color**: Gold (`#DAA520`)
- **Background**: Pink gradient (`#FFD1DC`)
- **Paper/cards**: Blush pink (`#FFF0F5`)
- **Text**: Purple (`#5A2D82`)
- **Font**: Brush Script MT with Comic Sans fallback
- **Special effects**: Gold shimmer, dotted background pattern, gradient buttons

<Info>
  The girlboss theme features unique styling including sparkle emojis in the theme selector, custom gold-bordered buttons, and a textured background for a distinctive journaling experience.
</Info>

## Changing your theme

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open settings">
    Click the settings icon (gear) in the sidebar drawer.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select theme">
    In the "Theme" dropdown menu, choose from:
    - **Light** - Bright, minimalist design
    - **Dark** - Comfortable for low light
    - **System** - Matches OS preference
    - **✨ Girlboss ✨** - Fun, empowering style
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Apply immediately">
    The theme applies instantly when selected. No save button required.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Theme features

All themes include:

### Glassmorphism effects

Cards, drawers, and app bars use semi-transparent backgrounds with blur effects for a modern, layered appearance:

```jsx
backgroundColor: alpha(theme.palette.background.paper, 0.5),
backdropFilter: 'blur(10px)',
WebkitBackdropFilter: 'blur(10px)',
```

### Dynamic monet colors

When viewing a journal entry with an attached image, the theme can extract dominant colors and apply them as accent colors throughout the UI (except in girlboss mode).

<CodeGroup>
```jsx Light mode monet
// In light mode, monet colors affect borders
borderColor: alpha(activeMonetColor, 0.5)
```

```jsx Dark mode monet
// In dark mode, monet colors tint backgrounds
backgroundColor: alpha(activeMonetColor, existingAlpha)
```
</CodeGroup>

### Emotion-based background flashes

When you save or update an entry, the background briefly flashes a color corresponding to the detected emotion:

- **Joy**: Warm, bright colors
- **Sadness**: Cool, muted tones
- **Anger**: Red-orange hues
- **Fear**: Purple tints
- **Surprise**: Yellow highlights

<Note>
  The flash effect lasts 1 second and uses a 30% opacity overlay to avoid disrupting your workflow.
</Note>

## Theme persistence

Your theme preference is saved to browser localStorage under the key `appThemeMode` and automatically loads when you restart the application.

<Info>
  Theme data is stored locally in your browser. If you clear browser data or use MoodJourney on a different device, you'll need to reselect your preferred theme.
</Info>
